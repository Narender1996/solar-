Services

Solar Solutions

Our products and solutions are made to meet all kinds of solar needs from 30W to 30MW.

Solar For Home

Many homes in India are going solar to have 24×7 power and to save on electricity bills. Depending on the electrical load that you wish to run on solar and your budget.

Solar For Offices

Many offices which are in metro cities run on Grid-tie solar system. This is a battery-less system that consists of grid-tie inverter and solar panels.

Solar For School & Universities

Many schools in metros opt for Grid-tie solar system. This is a battery-less system that consists of grid-tie inverter and solar panels.

Solar For Villages

DC system is apt for villages because it can operate fully on solar without any reliance on grid. It is a maintainence-free and easy-to-use system that comes with solar panels.
